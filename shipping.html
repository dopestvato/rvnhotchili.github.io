How is it Ship?

Delivery Rate Delivery Period
Baguio City Php 109.00 1-2 days ***
Outside Baguio City Php 139.00 2-4 days ***


Valid for domestic shipping via Xend.

***Based on delivery periods as published by Xend.

Please note that counting begins from ship out date.

Delivery periods are not guaranteed and exclude Sundays and Holidays.




  • Shipment Schedule

    • Orders are shipped Monday through Friday, excluding Philippine public holidays. Any orders placed on Saturday or Sunday will be shipped the following week.

  • Payment

    • We only accept Cash on Delivery

  • Courier

    • All domestic shipments are handled by Xend courier.

    **Provincial packages are forwarded to shipping partners such as 2Go, AP Cargo and Abest Express.

  • Tracking

    • Tracking information is encoded from the time your package reaches Xend's warehouse. This may take up to 24 hours from the date of ship out. To track your package, simply go to their website: http://new.xend.com.ph/ and input the tracking code provided to you via email. You may also reach Xend through any of the following channels:

    Tel no: 660-XEND (660-9363)

    Email: info@xend.com.ph

  • International Shipping

    • Currently not available.
